We are an organization that focuses on tech and social events. If you like video games, attending technology related events, coding, or playing board games then your search for a group to join has just ended. Check out our list of robotics/IoT gear that we have on hand for members to expirement with.

Events

The last Thursday of every month we host a game night. Game nights consist of large scale party games, board games, console video games, or LAN partys in the science building computer lab. Every year we attend Ohio LinuxFest in Columbus, Ohio. We host robot play days, trips to local conferences, disc golfing events at Pipestem Park, cookouts in Athen's Park, and a variety of social outings to local restaurants such as Campestre's.

Projects

We are currently collaborating with the Concord Geology Club on making an augmented reality sandbox. If you would like to get involved, send any of the officers an email requesting more information about the AR Sandbox Project!

Study Sessions

We host study sessions once a week in the science building computer lab. Come by to get advice on problems from upperclassmen and hangout with people while doing work. It's also the perfect time to work on group projects and come up with new ideas!

Be sure to like us on Facebook and join our group for notifications on upcoming events.

Also, share our website to get the word out that there is an awesome organization at Concord University!
